Our Story

Where the ocean
meets fine flavor

Perched at the edge of the Arabian Sea, The Pier is where the rhythm of the waves sets the pace. Our open-air bistro was born from a love of fresh coastal ingredients, slow meals, and the kind of conversations that linger long after sunset.

Every dish tells a story rooted in Goa's rich culinary heritage — spiced with Portuguese influence, softened by the sea breeze, and plated with care. From the morning catch to hand-pressed cocktails, every detail is considered.
